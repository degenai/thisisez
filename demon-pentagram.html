The thesis, both clauses load-bearing
The money is made by the trades, not the holding —
executed on assets you could hodl forever anyway.
Drop either clause and it breaks. Trades without hodlable assets = harvesting a melting ice cube: the edge is real and the book dies anyway (our best pure demon on paper is built from two decaying vol products — it beats "holding" only because holding them is a funeral). Hodlable assets without trades = a parked portfolio that eats none of its own volatility. The demon needs both: real assets that survive being forgotten, worked by a rebalancing discipline that converts their disagreements into compounding.

The pentagram — every edge a champion 12/12 universes · 12/12 hodl 100-world depth · frictions pending
Construction rule discovered the hard way: committees built for maximum compiled yield verify with regime holes — but a committee where every one of its ten pairwise edges independently survives all twelve universes composed into the first perfect card the campaign has ever produced. Five real, dividend-paying, boring assets. No leverage. No memes. Drawn with its chords, the topology is literally our sigil.
The ten edges (each independently tournament-tested across 12 universes × 250 worlds):
  • EWZ–GDX · GDX–XLU · EWZ–XLP · EWZ–XLU · XLP–XLU — perfect: 12/12 wins, 12/12 hodl (gold lines)
  • ECH–EWZ · ECH–GDX · ECH–XLP · ECH–XLU · GDX–XLP — 11–12/12 wins, 11–12/12 hodl (violet lines)

Why this is a serious candidate — and what's still unproven
Why it's interesting: yield can be computed from pairs (a committee's harvest is literally the weighted sum of its edges' spread variances — provable), but survival can't — and empirically, survival only composed when every edge was independently elite. Max-yield committees verified with regime holes; the all-edges-elite star swept. That makes the pentagram a construction principle, not a stock tip: five mutually-arguing real assets, every argument profitable, no single point of failure — and if any one node dies, you've lost a fifth of a book whose other six edges still work.

What's still unproven honest WIP: verification ran at screening depth (100 worlds/cell; deepening in progress); one historical window and one accounting basis; trading costs beyond spreads, taxes, and operational frictions (country-fund spreads are real) not yet modeled — a friction-physics test suite is designed and queued.
